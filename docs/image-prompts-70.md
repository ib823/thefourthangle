## Why 2400×1260?

- Exact 2× retina of the 1200×630 OG standard
- 1.91:1 = WhatsApp, Facebook, LinkedIn, Telegram OG previews
- Twitter (2:1) crops only 30px top/bottom — centered subject survives
- 1:1 thumbnail center-crops 1260×1260 — centered subject survives
- Downsamples cleanly: hero (960×504), card (480×480), thumb (240×240)
- Push notifications, desktop, tablet, mobile, watch — zero crop loss
